# Supremacy - Game Overview

## Goal
Build and defend a network of planetary colonies against an AI opponent. One player wins by controlling all planets in the system.

## Core Mechanics
- Starts with 1 colonized planet each (player vs AI) on an initial planet
- Intermediate planets are uninhabited—both must terraform and colonize them
- Balancing resources is the main challenge: food, minerals, fuel, energy, taxes, morale, population

## Resources & Economy
| Resource | Source/Use |
|----------|-------------|
| Food | Farming stations (surface) or cargo ships |
| Minerals | Mining stations (surface) |
| Fuel | Produced from minerals; powers all ships |
| Energy | Solar satellites (orbit) + cargo transfer |

## The 8 Screens

1. **Home Screen** - View all planets, select individual ones
2. **Docking Bay** - Manage ships: launch, send to other planets, land
3. **Purchase** - Buy ships/satellites: fighters, bombers, cruisers, transports, cargo haulers, farming stations, mining stations, solar satellites, terraformers (limit 1)
4. **Planet Details** - View/set planet name, credits, resources, population, morale, tax rate
5. **Military** - Create/train platoons with armor/weapons; control troop aggression
6. **Cargo Control** - Load/unload food, minerals, fuel, energy to/from ships
7. **Battle** - View military strength per platoon; manage combat on enemy's planet
8. **Save/Load** - Game persistence

## Turn System
- Everything runs on a timer (real-time simulation)
- Populations grow (affected by morale & tax rate), resources fluctuate, cargo transfers

## AI Opponent
- 4 difficulty levels: each builds its own colonies, terraforms planets, attempts conquest once strong enough
